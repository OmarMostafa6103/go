Businesses & Shippers

GoLynk is an AI-powered logistics platform that intelligently routes shipments through a network of existing vehicles, trains, microhubs, and lockers—delivering faster, cheaper, and with significantly lower emissions than traditional courier services. We orchestrate unused capacity to create a woven, efficient logistics web.
Today, logistics is fragmented and wasteful: trucks run 20–35% empty, cars operate at only 30% capacity, and shippers pay premium rates for inefficient routing. GoLynk matches shipments to available capacity in real time, eliminating waste and reducing costs for everyone.
Traditional couriers own vehicles and charge fixed rates. GoLynk doesn't own vehicles; instead, we coordinate shipments across existing capacity (trucks, trains, vans, bikes) and microhubs, which enables lower costs, more flexibility, and no new infrastructure—just smarter coordination.

Travel Participants & Carriers

A shipper enters pickup/drop-off details; our AI matches the shipment to available capacity (direct or multi-hop via microhubs/lockers). The shipment moves along that capacity with live tracking and event updates—no detours, just efficient routing.
Multimodal means a shipment can use multiple transport types—truck, train, van, bike, or pedestrian legs—whichever is most efficient for that route, enabling cost and emissions savings.
Microhubs are small physical consolidation points (corner shops, kiosks, train stations, depots) where shipments temporarily consolidate and wait for the next efficient leg, enabling dynamic rerouting and cost savings.
A dynamic locker is a smart parcel locker enabling contactless 24/7 handovers between carriers, shippers, and recipients, eliminating coordination overhead and providing flexible pickup/drop-off across cities.
Our AI analyzes location, timing, capacity, compliance, and detour tolerance to match shipments with compatible journeys automatically, learning from every shipment to improve match quality continuously.
We provide live GPS tracking and event-level updates at each leg (pickup, microhub arrival, vehicle boarding, final delivery) with notifications at key milestones and frequently updating ETAs.

Carbon & ESG / Environment

By filling empty vehicle space and eliminating unnecessary trips, GoLynk reduces CO₂ per package by 40–70% vs traditional dedicated delivery; multimodal routing and microhubs further cut footprint.
We calculate CO₂ savings per shipment vs a conventional alternative, aggregate results into auditable reports, and provide third-party verifiable Scope 3-ready data.
We produce auditable CO₂ savings reports suitable for CSRD and ESG reporting that are third-party verifiable and support corporate sustainability commitments.
