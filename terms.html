1. Definitions

Platform
The GoLynk orchestration software and related interfaces, including web and API access points, used to coordinate and match shipment demand to available transport capacity.
Shipper
An entity submitting shipment or logistics demand to the Platform, including e-commerce operators, retailers, B2B logistics users, and individuals.
Carrier
An independent transport operator providing available route capacity, including trucking companies, logistics operators, van services, and alternative transport providers.
Capacity Partner
A fleet operator, microhub operator, logistics facility, or infrastructure participant contributing capacity, storage, or coordination services to the Platform.
Corridor
A structured, algorithmically validated lane or route segment connecting two or more geographic points, configured for coordinated deployment of shipments across participating Carriers.
Pilot Services
Limited-scope deployment of orchestration services within defined geographic areas or specific Corridors prior to full commercial rollout.

2. Nature of the Service

GoLynk provides a coordination and orchestration layer that matches shipment demand to existing routes based on algorithmic scoring, corridor density, SLA weighting, deviation thresholds, and other operational variables.

Not a Carrier

GoLynk is not itself a carrier and does not assume responsibility for physical transport unless explicitly agreed in writing as part of a separate service agreement. Transport execution is performed by independent Carriers or Capacity Partners, who remain solely responsible for safe, lawful, and compliant delivery.

No Assumption of Possession

GoLynk does not take physical possession of goods unless separately contracted. The Platform facilitates matching; it does not assume custody, insurance, or liability for goods in transit.

Coordination Role

The Platform coordinates deployment logistics between Shippers and Carriers. No agency, partnership, or employment relationship exists between GoLynk and Participants unless expressly agreed in writing.
