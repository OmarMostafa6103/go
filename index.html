Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

GoLynk is an AI-powered logistics platform that intelligently routes shipments through a network of existing vehicles, trains, microhubs, and lockersdelivering faster, cheaper, and with significantly lower emissions than traditional courier services. We orchestrate unused capacity to create a woven, efficient logistics web.
Today, logistics is fragmented and wasteful: trucks run 2035% empty, cars operate at only 30% capacity, and shippers pay premium rates for inefficient routing. GoLynk matches shipments to available capacity in real time, eliminating waste and reducing costs for everyone.
Traditional couriers own vehicles and charge fixed rates. GoLynk doesn't own vehicles; instead, we coordinate shipments across existing capacity (trucks, trains, vans, bikes) and microhubs, which enables lower costs, more flexibility, and no new infrastructurejust smarter coordination.
Shippers (e-commerce, retailers, B2B and individuals), logistics operators & 3PLs (fleets with unused capacity), and microhub & retail partners (shops, kiosks, lockers). All three benefit from the same intelligent orchestration engine.
Most shippers save 3050% on delivery costs compared to traditional couriers. Savings depend on shipment type, frequency, and corridor; flexible timing typically yields the highest savings.
